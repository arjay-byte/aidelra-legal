Data Deletion Instructions

If you want us to remove your data from the AIDELra WhatsApp automation, please follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Email arjaypenaflorida1@gmail.com with the subject: Data deletion request.
  2. Include the phone number that interacted with the WhatsApp service and a short description of what you want deleted.
  3. We will respond within 30 days with confirmation and, where possible, remove your personal data from our systems and logs.

Notes

Certain logs required for legal or security reasons may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form. If you have additional questions, contact us at the email above.
