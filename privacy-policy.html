Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-28

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AIDELra ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you interact with our WhatsApp automation powered by n8n.

What data we collect

  • WhatsApp messages: Messages you send to the phone number connected to the WhatsApp Business account. These are temporarily processed by n8n to perform the automation/workflow tasks you requested.
  • Metadata: Timestamps, phone numbers, and delivery status required to route messages and track delivery.
  • Optional: Any data you explicitly provide through forms or flows (e.g., name, email) if the workflow asks for it.

How we use the data

We use data to perform the automation and deliver the experience you expect (e.g., respond to commands, provide information, trigger workflows). We do not sell your data.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • WhatsApp / Meta: message transport and business APIs.
  • n8n: workflow automation where steps of the automation run (hosted by you or by n8n Cloud depending on your setup).

How long we keep data

We retain messages and metadata only as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. If you request deletion (see Data Deletion page), we will remove your data as described.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure — use caution when sharing sensitive data.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data. See our Data Deletion instructions for how to submit a request.
